About Us

Manchester Grammar School (MGS) is a prestigious independent day school for boys located in Manchester, England. The school was founded in 1515 and has a long history of academic excellence and innovation.

MGS is known for its rigorous academic program, which includes a wide range of subjects across various disciplines, including humanities, sciences, languages, and the arts. The school is consistently ranked as one of the top schools in the UK, and its students regularly achieve outstanding results in national and international exams.
